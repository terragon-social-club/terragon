Become a Founding Member

Terragon is the home of American culture online. All Americans are welcome, irrespective of race, creed or color. If you’re a United States citizen who loves your country and your countrymen, we welcome you to join today.

Your contribution will go directly to the Terragon Social Club. We spend money on servers, engineers, designers, and very light marketing. Our goal is for the Club to be 100% self-sufficient, with no need to serve ads to our members. All of the intellectual property that comprises the Terragon Social Club (code, design assets, etc) will always be available publicly in our Github repositories.

As a founding member, you will get a spiffy badge next to your name, access to exclusive content, and lifetime access to Terragon. You will also be helping to ensure that silicon valley loses its monopoly on discussion and information about current events and American culture.
